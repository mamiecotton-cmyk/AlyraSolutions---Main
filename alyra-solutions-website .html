About Us

Built by service industry
insiders, for owners

Alyra Solutions was founded with a single mission: give every service business the software infrastructure that enterprise companies take for granted.

Our Mission

Alyra Solutions builds cloud-based software platforms tailored specifically to service industry businesses. We believe every salon owner, restaurant operator, barber, and spa director deserves software that was designed for their world — not adapted from something else.

What We Build

We develop and operate a suite of multi-tenant SaaS platforms, each purpose-built for a specific segment of the service industry:

  • Alyra Nail Salon Software — appointment management, virtual color try-on, and inventory for nail salons
  • Alyra Restaurant Solutions — online ordering, reservations, POS integration, and payments for restaurants
  • Alyra Barbershop Solutions — scheduling, chair management, and client tracking for barbershops
  • Alyra Spa Solutions — booking, memberships, and guest experience tools for spas and wellness centers

How Our Platform Works

Alyra Solutions operates as a multi-tenant SaaS platform. Each business that subscribes to an Alyra product receives a fully isolated, secure tenant environment within our shared infrastructure. Business owners manage their own data, customers, and payments independently.

Payment processing is facilitated through Stripe Connect, which allows each tenant to receive payouts directly into their connected Stripe account. Alyra Solutions acts as the platform operator and does not hold, commingle, or take custody of tenant funds beyond the applicable platform service fee.

Payment & Fund Flow

Our platform uses Stripe Connect to enable service businesses to collect payments from their customers. Here is how funds flow:

  • Customers pay directly through the business's Alyra-powered storefront or booking page
  • Funds are collected and processed by Stripe on behalf of the connected business account (the tenant)
  • Alyra Solutions collects a platform service fee per transaction
  • Net funds are paid out directly to the tenant's bank account on Stripe's standard payout schedule
