About Us

Built by service industry
insiders, for owners

Alyra Solutions was founded with a single mission: give every service business the software infrastructure that enterprise companies take for granted.

Our Mission

Alyra Solutions builds cloud-based software platforms tailored specifically to service industry businesses. We believe every salon owner, restaurant operator, barber, and spa director deserves software designed for their world — not adapted from something else.

What We Build

We develop and operate a suite of multi-tenant SaaS platforms, each purpose-built for a specific segment of the service industry:

  • Alyra Nail Salon Software — appointment management, virtual color try-on, and inventory
  • Alyra Restaurant Solutions — online ordering, POS integration, and payments
  • Alyra Barbershop Solutions — scheduling, chair management, and client tracking
  • Alyra Spa Solutions — booking, memberships, and guest experience tools

How Our Platform Works

Alyra Solutions operates as a multi-tenant SaaS platform. Each business receives a fully isolated, secure tenant environment. Payment processing is facilitated through Stripe Connect, allowing each tenant to receive payouts directly into their connected Stripe account. Alyra Solutions acts as the platform operator and does not hold or commingle tenant funds beyond the applicable platform service fee.

Payment & Fund Flow

  • Customers pay through the business's Alyra-powered storefront or booking page
  • Funds are collected and processed by Stripe on behalf of the connected business (tenant)
  • Alyra Solutions collects a platform service fee per transaction
  • Net funds are paid out directly to the tenant's bank account on Stripe's standard payout schedule
